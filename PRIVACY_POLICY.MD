# PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: June 21, 2026

## 1. INTRODUCTION

Staff.fm ("we," "our," or "us") is a custom Discord moderation and content management bot (the "Service") for the 
Last.fm Discord server ("Lastcord") at discord.gg/lastfm. The service is not intended for use outside Lastcord.

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not 
agree with our policies and practices, do not join the Last.fm Discord.

## 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information that is automatically collected when you use our Service.

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We may collect the following personal information when you interact with our Service, specifically upon verifying your 
Last.fm account after joining the Last.fm Discord server:

- Your Discord User ID and username
- Your Last.fm username and public profile information (Display name, country)

In addition, we may collect:
- Your interactions with our Service (commands used, Staffmail messages in the bot's DMs)

Note about the anonymous Staffmail feature: If you choose to use the anonymous staffmail feature, your messages will be
sent anonymously to our support team. Your Discord ID, username or avatar will not be visible to the support team but is 
saved to the database to facilitate replies to your message.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information:

- Your public messages sent in the Last.fm Discord server (for logging and moderation purposes)

### 2.3 How We Collect Information

We collect information through Discord's API when you interact with our Service in the Last.fm Discord server and 
through Last.fm's API if you have provided us with your username. We do not collect information from any other sources.

## 3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect exclusively for purposes related to providing and improving our Service within the 
Last.fm Discord server, including:
- Verifying your Last.fm account and assigning appropriate roles
- Moderating the Last.fm Discord server and enforcing community guidelines
- Responding to your inquiries and support requests
- Providing utilities such as scheduled discussions or self-assignable temporary mutes

## 4. SHARING YOUR INFORMATION

We do not share your personal information with any third parties, except as required by law.

Your personal data may be processed outside your country of residence, including in jurisdictions that may have
different data protection laws. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses
(SCCs) or equivalent legal protections to protect your data.

### 5. DATA RETENTION

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this 
Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Our retention periods are based on:

- The nature of the data and the purpose for which it was collected
- Legal, regulatory, or contractual requirements
- The need to resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

Generally, we retain:
- Your Discord User ID and username: For as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services
- Your present and past Last.fm username and public profile information: For as long as your account is active or as 
  needed to provide services
- Your joins and leaves of the Last.fm Discord server: For as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services 
- Your messages and interactions with our Service: For as long as your account is active or as needed to provide 
  services (your messages to the staffmail features are retained until the staffmail thread is closed)
- Your public messages sent in the Last.fm Discord server: 2 days 
- Logs and security data: 30 days

When personal data is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it in accordance with our data retention policies.

### 6. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies on our Service.

### 7. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

You have the following rights regarding your personal information
- Right to view: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you and receive a copy of it.
- Right to correct: You can request to correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information we hold about you.
- Right to request deletion: You can request to request deletion your personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below. We will 
verify your identity before processing your request. We aim to respond within 30 days (or 45 days where required by 
law). If you disagree with our response, you may request an appeal or lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

### 8. DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against 
unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

- Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS protocols
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data stored in our databases
- Regular security assessments and updates
- Restricted access to personal information on a need-to-know basis (only moderators and administrators have access to 
the moderation commands, only the bot owner has access to the database)

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use 
commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

### 9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA/UK). We do not knowingly collect 
personal information from children under the applicable age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child 
has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we become aware that we have collected 
personal information from a child without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information.
